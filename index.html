Filipino Games

Some Famous Filipno Games many children played before technology took over.

Picture Game Description
Patintero There are 2 teams, offense and defense. The defense's job is to stop the offense from getting to the other side. Once the defense tags the offense, the defense gets a point and the offense is out.
Kadang-Kadang This game usually involves 4 players and 2 pairs of bamboo stilts. The player balances themselves on the bamboo stilts and race to the finish line. There are many ways to play this game, you may use coconut shells and/or wood.
Piko The hopscoth of the Philippines. Can be played with many people or yourself. First, players will draw a line on the floor. The first player will throw a "pamato" at the first number. After jumping to the end of the numbers, they will go back and pick the pamato and repeat the process till all numbers are complete.
Luksong Baka One person will be bending over while the others will jump over them. The one bending over is the "baka". The players musn't touch or fall on the "baka", if they do they will take their place.
Sipa "Sipa" in English is kick. This agme requires you to kick the toy without making it touch the floor.
